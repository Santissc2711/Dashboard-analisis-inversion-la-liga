README

Available Data:
	You'll find a folder with the result matches for the last ten years in "La Liga".
	Also, you'll find a file called "investments.csv" with the current costs for sponsorships and the profit by team for every goal.

LaLiga File Structure:

	Record:
		Round,Date,Team 1,FT,HT,Team 2
		?,(Fri) 19 Aug 2016 (33),RCD La Coruña (1),2-1,0-0,SD Eibar (1)

	Descriptions:
		Round - Discard
		Date -  (Day of week of match) Date (week of year) Eg. (Fri) 19 Aug 2016 (33) 
		Team 1 - Team (Round) Eg. RCD La Coruña (1) .. This is the Home team
		FT - Score at Final Time E.g. 2-1
		HT - Score at Half Time  E.g. 0-0
		Team 2 - Team (Round) E.g. SD Eibar (1) .. This is the Away team
